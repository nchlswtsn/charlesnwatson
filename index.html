Summary

I am currently a full-stack developer in the San Francisco Bay Area. Using the MEAN stack to build personal and client projects, I develop everything from small framework modules to full-stack web and mobile applications.

I have worked extensively on solo projects as well as teams ranging from 2 developers up to 6 developers. My experience is primarily centered around Agile-Scrum environments and I enjoy the collaborative team learning opportunity.
